  1. I was born!

    March 23rd, 1993

    On the snowy 23rd day of March, 1993, I was born in 郑州 Zhengzhou, Henan, China

  2. Primary school

    September, 2000

    On which day I couldn't remember by now, I attended the 伊河路小学 in the September of the second millennium

  3. Junior high school

    September, 2006

    The whole month was filled with anxiety, excitement and various possibilities of my future life. I guess that I wasn't taught to be sad about the past. Luckily, my first graduation ended with joy and relief: I was going to study at 郑州外国语中学 Zhengzhou Foreign Language Middle School, class 0906

  4. High school

    September, 2009

    After the admittance exam of 郑州外国语学校 Zhengzhou Foreign Language School, I went to run in the water fountain and got all soaked up before I gave a hug to my middle school teachers. My family and I traveled to 四川 Sichuan the following day and I got my scores on the train back home. Oh my classes were 1204 for my first year and 1206 after I chose the 理科 science branch
