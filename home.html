  6. Cornell summer school

    June 27th, 2010

    A hot summer in Ithaca, NY, with the big clock tower chimes every hour. I spent my first summer in the US. and took English for Academic Purposes and Introductory to Economics. Thank you Big Red and professor Kris Kershaw.
    Dear Kevin,
    When I lived in Ithaca, I could hear the Cornell chimes when my windows were open. Now I live about 10 miles south, & the chimes are one of the few things I miss. Why is it the Middle Autumn Festival? Here, Autumn doesn't begin until the autumnal equinox, Sept. 23 this year. The full moon of September is called the Harvest Moon. Your writing has certainly improved. That will make it much easier for me to write you a good recommendation ... Why wouldn't I remember you? I enjoyed having you as a student, and I was blown away by your yearbook. ("Blown away" means really, really impressed.) It's something I will treasure forever. Thanks for writing. It was good to hear from you!

    Best regards,
    Kris Kershaw
